The Owner

Clive Crerar

Clive is a veteran of the motor industry.
He has worked at every level and at various motor manufacturers since 1976.
Clive was a mechanic at Nissan and Volkswagen in his early years. He later on worked as a Workshop manager at Nissan, after which he worked as a production line supervisor at Ford motor companies Land Rover division.
Following this he oversaw the land rover imports at Motor Via, his role was to ensure that all land rovers imported from the manufacturer was prepared for distribution to all major dealers across the country. In the recent years Clive was in charge of special vehicle operations at TFM where special orders of Land Rovers and other vehicles were prepared for roles such as medical vehicles, Defense force vehicles and game viewing.
Clive now runs his own business selling parts and repairing vehicles out of his own capacity.

About The business

TQ Bike & 4x4 accessories is a retailer of quality automotive accessories that will enhance the functionality and appearance of your motorcycle or 4x4.
Our parts are locally manufactured and are widely used in the off road community.
Our supplier understands the importance of design engineering when it comes to quality, efficiency, durability and practicality.
You will mail your parts to you at a calculated cost, depending on where you are situated. Our prices do not include delivery cost.
